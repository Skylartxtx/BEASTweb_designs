WHITE TIGER

WHITE TIGER is often depicted as a fierce and powerful beast that guards the West. In Chinese mythology, it is associated with the five elements and is believed to possess courage and valor. White Tiger is considered a god of mountains and forests, and is believed to have the ability to ward off evil spirits and protect humans from harm. It is also associated with the autumn season and is often revered as a symbol of strength and ferocity.
