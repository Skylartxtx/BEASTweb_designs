INTRODUCTION

Azure Dragon

is one of the divine beasts in traditional Chinese culture, often depicted as a giant dragon with a blue or green body. Azure Dragon represents the East and symbolizes power, dignity, longevity, and auspiciousness. In traditional Chinese Feng Shui, Azure Dragon is often considered as a guardian spirit for protecting homes, warding off evil spirits, and bringing blessings.
