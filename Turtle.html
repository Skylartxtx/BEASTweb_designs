INTRODUCTION

BLACK TORTOISE

BLACK TORTOISE also known as Black Warrior, is one of the divine beasts in traditional Chinese culture, often depicted as a giant black or dark-colored turtle-snake hybrid. Black Tortoise represents the North and symbolizes longevity, stability, and the balance of yin and yang. In Chinese mythology, Black Tortoise is seen as a water god and earth spirit, often used as a symbol for protecting homes, warding off evil spirits, and dispelling negative energy.
