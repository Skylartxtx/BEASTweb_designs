How was this website born

The four divine beasts hold a high position in traditional Chinese culture. They guard the four directions geographically, bless all living beings in the cycle of time, and represent good luck, peace, joy, and good wishes. Although we are not superstitious, the images of the Four Gods are still so beautiful and a crystallization of wisdom, which should be understood and appreciated by future generations. However, due to various obstacles such as language, culture, geographical location, damage, etc., the treasures enshrined in museums are difficult for people from all over the world to appreciate. Adhering to the two basic principles of "cultural equality" and "national is the world", we hope to use this website to bring you auspicious blessings and revive the treasure.

I hope you can learn more about traditional culture through this website, and also hope to spread traditional Chinese aesthetics through cultivating sentiment, so as not to let the pearl dust.
